Overview

 Sync has been created for Hank Air Management to support an efficient scheduling process.
Sync is an application that will assist managers to release schedules in a more organized and timely manner. Djs will have the ability to submit their availabilities and managers will be able view all availabilities all on one platform.

User Sign in and Login

Enter your Login Information given by your admin. Enter your Username in the space labeled “Username” Enter your Password in the space labeled “Password” Click the Login Button

Manager Instructions

A manager will create users’ accounts for all existing and new managers and Djs. They will have the capability to delete any manager or Dj accounts.
A manager will also have the capability to create and manage schedules. They will also be able to accept and decline shift change requests from the Djs.
